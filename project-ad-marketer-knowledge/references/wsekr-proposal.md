# WSEKR / Education Lead Generation Proposal Reference

## Advertiser Context

Wall Street English Korea is a premium adult English education brand with offline centers, online/OCC business, Naver/Google/Meta operation, HubSpot CRM, and Power BI reporting environment.

The RFP intent is not simple media buying. They are looking for a long-term partner that can improve Naver performance, restructure accounts, run systematic A/B tests, respond to Naver AI-based ad changes, and suggest domestic media such as Kakao.

## Recommended Strategic Frame

The proposal should say:

- Naver performance stagnation is a structural issue, not only a bid issue.
- Key variables: search intent, account grouping, ADVoost expanded search, landing URL, ad copy, lead quality, CRM feedback, and Naver search-result trust environment.
- The primary proposal should be Naver performance redesign.
- Kakao, GFA, Power Content, Communication Ads, and blog/cafe exposure should be limited tests or supporting channels.

## Recommended Deck Flow

1. WSEKR issue diagnosis
2. RFP interpretation
3. Naver search environment changes
4. Current hypothesis: intent, structure, landing, lead quality, trust signals
5. Account restructure strategy
6. ADVoost/expanded-search operation loop
7. Keyword and ad-copy examples
8. Landing/CRO and conversion quality
9. Daiad Pro rank-efficiency testing
10. Naver ecosystem expansion: Power Content, GFA, Communication Ads
11. Kakao lead test strategy
12. Reporting/collaboration: HubSpot, Power BI, Asana, bilingual reporting
13. References and GabiaCNS capability
14. Data request and next-step schedule

## Keyword Strategy

Do not only show generic keywords. Use intent-based groups:

| Group | Example Keywords | Landing Direction | Role |
|---|---|---|---|
| Adult/worker | 성인영어학원, 성인영어회화, 직장인영어회화, 직장인영어학원 | Adult English / consultation | Core demand |
| Online/OCC | 화상영어, 성인화상영어, 온라인영어회화, 성인온라인영어학원 | OCC / online course | Online demand |
| Business English | 비즈니스영어, 비즈니스영어회화, 비즈니스영어학원 | Business English / B2B | High-value demand |
| Region/center | 강남영어회화, 강남영어회화학원, 종로영어회화, 부산영어회화 | Center pages | Visit/reservation demand |
| Level test/consultation | 영어회화레벨테스트, 영어무료레벨테스트, 영어상담 | Level test / consultation | Lead capture |
| Purpose-based phrases | 주말 직장인을 위한 영어학원, 영어로만 수업하는 성인 영어학원 추천 | Matching content/landing | High-intent discovery |

Exclude or treat cautiously:

- Youth/entrance exam intent: 유아, 초등, 중등, 고등, 수능, 입시, 학생, 토익 only when not relevant.
- Very broad intent: 영어, 영어학원, 영어회화 if budget or lead quality is weak.
- Weak self-study intent: 영어책, 독학, 공부법, 무료자료, 스터디모임, 앱 unless paired with service intent.

## Account Structure Principle

Do not split campaigns endlessly. Use the minimum intent units that allow control over:

- Search intent
- Landing URL
- Ad-copy promise
- Bid/rank strategy
- Conversion quality
- CRM feedback

Suggested structure:

- Brand
- Adult/worker
- Online/OCC
- Business English
- Region/center
- Level test/consultation
- Purpose/question keywords
- Test/expanded search discovery

## HubSpot / Power BI Positioning

Do not pretend GabiaCNS owns CRM operations. Explain R&R clearly:

- WSEKR owns lead-stage definitions and CRM data.
- GabiaCNS uses available feedback to optimize media, keyword, landing, and copy.
- Key quality signals: simple inquiry, consultation connected, reservation/visit, registration, revenue.
- If MQL/SQL terminology is used, explain it simply for Korean stakeholders.

## Kakao and Blog/Cafe Response

If WSEKR asks about Kakao channel or blog/cafe:

- Do not repeat too much that channel/account management is outside scope if already communicated.
- Propose available paid ad products.
- Frame them as limited validation, not guaranteed performance.

Recommended wording:

> 카카오와 블로그·카페 영역은 단순 채널 운영보다 광고 성과 개선을 위한 보조 접점으로 접근하는 것이 적합합니다. 가비아CNS는 카카오 비즈보드, 참여형 광고, 네이버 파워컨텐츠, 커뮤니케이션 애드 등 유료 광고 상품을 활용해 신규 리드 발굴과 검색광고 전환 보조 역할을 검증하겠습니다. 단, 모든 확장 매체는 즉시 대규모 집행이 아니라 CPA, 상담 연결률, 센터 방문/예약 전환, 리드 품질을 기준으로 단계적 테스트 후 확대 여부를 판단하겠습니다.
