# Kakao, Naver GFA, Power Content, and Content Placements

## Core Rule

Use Kakao, GFA, Communication Ads, Power Content, and blog/cafe placements as **supporting tests** unless the advertiser explicitly asks for awareness or has enough budget/data to support multi-channel operation.

Do not present them as a guaranteed solution for performance decline.

## Kakao Products

### Bizboard

Use for broad reach and lead-entry support in KakaoTalk and Kakao inventory. It can connect to Kakao landing actions and business solutions.

Use when:

- The client wants domestic reach.
- A simple lead action exists.
- Pixel/SDK or conversion tracking is available.
- There is enough budget for a meaningful test.

### Bizboard CPT

Use for high-impact time-based exposure. More branding-oriented. Do not recommend as default performance media unless campaign goal is reach/impact.

### Message Ads / Brand Message / Brand Pick

Use conditionally:

- Existing Kakao channel friend base exists.
- Message consent and audience rules are clear.
- Offer or CTA is strong.
- Fatigue and frequency are controlled.

For WSEKR:

- Channel operation is not the main scope.
- Message ads can be a reactivation test or lead nurture test.
- Judge by click rate, CPL, consultation connection, reservation/visit, and HubSpot stage quality.

### Engagement Ads + Business Form+

Good fit for lead collection:

- Level test request
- Consultation request
- Free trial/application
- Survey-style qualification

Use as a 1-2 month pilot before scaling.

## Naver GFA

Use as a support channel, not a Powerlink replacement, unless the brief is awareness.

Potential roles:

- Reach new interest audiences
- Re-expose brand after search or content touchpoints
- Test creative response
- Support branded search lift

Do not over-target. Too narrow targeting can reduce reach and learning.

## Communication Ads

Communication Ads can expose native-style messages in Naver communication surfaces such as cafes and topic-related contexts.

Use when:

- The client needs to reach interest communities.
- Topics match education, self-development, job/work, language learning, business English, study abroad, or career improvement.
- It is a limited test with CPL and lead quality criteria.

For WSEKR:

> 커뮤니케이션 애드는 영어학습·자기개발·직장인 관심 카페 지면에서 제한 테스트로 활용하고, CPL·상담 연결률·리드 품질 기준으로 확대 또는 중단을 판단합니다.

## Power Content

Power Content should not be proposed simply because the client has a blog.

Use only when:

- A permitted Power Content keyword exists.
- Blog/Post/Cafe content matches search intent.
- Content is informational and credible.
- The goal is exploration-stage trust support, not guaranteed direct conversion.

For WSEKR, candidate themes:

- Adult English learning
- Business English
- Region/center-based English conversation
- Level test and consultation
- "How to choose an adult English academy"
- "English academy for workers"

Recommended wording:

> 파워컨텐츠는 블로그 운영 대행이 아니라, 검색광고 전환 전 탐색 단계의 신뢰 콘텐츠를 보강하는 제한 테스트로 제안합니다. 실제 콘텐츠와 연결 가능한 허용 키워드만 선별하고, 상담 전환 또는 보조 전환 기여가 확인될 때 확대하겠습니다.

## Brand Search Synergy Report

Use conditionally when budget/report conditions are met.

Purpose:

- Check whether DA exposure increased brand-related search.
- Check whether search ads gained additional clicks after branding exposure.
- Evaluate DA not only by direct conversions, but by search demand lift.

For WSEKR, useful if they already spend significant budget on Naver and want to justify DA.

Do not say DA is necessary only because the budget is high. Say:

> 조건 충족 시 브랜드서치 시너지 리포트를 활용해 GFA 노출 이후 WSEKR 관련 검색과 검색광고 추가 클릭이 증가하는지 확인하겠습니다.
